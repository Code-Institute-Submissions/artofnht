Nick Hope-Thomson

Nick grew up in a ‘70s village feel’ South Norwood in London. His fascination with heroes was first sparked when he saw ‘Star Wars, A New Hope’ at the cinema as a young boy, and it grew from there. Nick discovered he could pick up a comic for just 10p in the local shop, and his interest amplified into a huge passion.

Nick soon realised he could visualise his own thoughts of his much-loved heroes through pencil, pen and paper and so his comic adaptations were born and his zest for drawing eventually developed into completing a Diploma in Graphic Design.

For the last two decades, Nick has produced varied commissions for friends, family and acquaintances along with online clients. Nick is a walking encyclopedia when it comes to all things X-Men and Avengers, and his favourite Superhero is Phoenix. Nick is also keen on caricaturing people as Superheroes and drawing portraits.

If you are interested in commissioning Nick for a Superhero illustration or a caricature or portrait of yourself or loved ones, please contact him via the contact form, or by email at nickhopet@hotmail.com.

If you wish to view further artwork of Nick’s, please visit Nick's artist page on comicartfans.com.
